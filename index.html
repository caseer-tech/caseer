Build a Professional GRC Portfolio

Practical deliverables to showcase your skills.

1. Risk Assessment Report

Conduct a comprehensive risk assessment for a simulated enterprise, identifying assets, threats, and vulnerabilities.

2. Information Security Policies

Draft core security policies (e.g., Access Control or Data Classification) aligned with industry best practices.

3. Information Security Risk Register

Develop and maintain a structured information security risk register using industry-standard frameworks to ensure comprehensive visibility and proactive mitigation strategies.

4. Compliance Gap Assessment

Evaluate compliance against selected frameworks and recommend improvements.

4-Week Program Structure

An intensive journey to master essential GRC skills.

Week 1

Governance & Frameworks

Understand the foundations of IT governance, explore key frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001), and learn how to align security strategy with business objectives.

Week 2

Risk Assessment Essentials

Dive into risk management methodologies. Learn to identify, assess, and document risks using qualitative and quantitative approaches.

Week 3

Compliance & Auditing

Navigate complex regulatory landscapes. Practice mapping controls to compliance requirements and conducting gap analyses.

Week 4

Vendor Risk & Portfolio Review

Master third-party risk management. Finalize your deliverables and participate in a comprehensive portfolio review.
